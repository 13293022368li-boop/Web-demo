About Our Cafe

Tucked away in the heart of Palmerston North, Coffee Cafe has been brewing connections and great coffee since 2015. We're more than just a place to grab a cup — we're where friends meet, ideas spark, and neighbors feel at home.

Our beans are roasted locally, our pastries come fresh out of the oven each morning, and our team is dedicated to creating a cozy, welcoming space for everyone who walks through our doors.

Eco-friendly practices and locally sourced ingredients
